Event Overview

Afriski Mountain Resort welcomes adventure seekers
to experience the thrill of winter sports.
Our programme includes skiing, snowboarding, and tubing.
Join us for an unforgettable winter peak experience.

The event schedule is designed for all skill levels.
Beginners can join quided ski lessons on Day 1.
Advanced athletes can compete in snowboarding challenges.
Fun activities like tubing wrap up the programme on Dat 3
