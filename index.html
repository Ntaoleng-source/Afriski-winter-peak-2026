Event Overview

The Afriski Winter Sports Event is Lesotho's premier ski and snowboard festival,
Attracting adventure seekers from around the world to experience snow sports in Africa's highest mountains.
Join the Afriski Winter Peak 2026, a thrilling mountain biking event set against Lesotho's stunning winter landscapes!
Challenge your skills on scenic trails, soak in the breathtaking views, and connect with fellow biking enthusiants.
Whether you're a pro or a beginner, all levels are welcome. Expect epic trails, fun vibes, and unforgettable moments.
Mark your calenders for an adventure like no other in the mountains of Lesotho!

Experience world-class instructors quiding you through exhilarating downhill runs.
Enjoy cozy mountain lodges with traditional Lesotho hospitality.
Capture unforgettable moments with breathtaking sunrise ski sessions.
